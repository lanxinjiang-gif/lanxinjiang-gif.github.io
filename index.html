Lanxin Jiang

Assistant Professor of Accounting

College of Business, Stony Brook University

My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and accounting, with a particular focus on how AI and machine learning can improve the accuracy and timeliness of detecting misconduct, including material misstatements, abnormal accruals, and financial statement fraud. My work aims to bridge the gap between traditional auditing practices and emerging technological innovations.
